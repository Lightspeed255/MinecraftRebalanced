# Minecraft Rebalanced

A datapack + resourcepack aimed at rebalancing the minecraft progression system. Made for Minecraft 26.1.2. Heavily inspired by the video by Emerald Quartz of the same name.

FEATURES:
- New ore progression
  - Wood and stone are now the same tier (stone having slightly better stats)
  - Copper replaces where stone was in the progression.
  - Gold now replaces diamonds in the overworld, with diamonds being pushed to the nether, and ancient debris to the end islands.
  - Obsidian can now be mined by gold tools.
  - This locks the overpowered netherite gear to the post game.
  - Diamonds are given a new texture to fit the nether.
  - Ore generation has been overhauled completely, with gold essentially replacing how diamonds generated before.
  - Nether gold ore now drops one raw gold.
  - Blocks of ore require the tool of that ore or higher to be mined.
  - Loot tables are changed in the overworld to fit the new progression.
    
- Protection nerfs
  - Protection has been nerfed from providing 4% per level to 2.5% (1 piece of full prot 4 from 16% -> 10%, Full prot 4 armor from 64% -> 40%). (Not enabled at the moment. Please wait for the next update.)
  - A new protection style enchantment similar to projectile protection, blast protection and fire protection has been added. Slashing protection.
  - This enchantment reduces damage from all axe and sword attacks, pve and pvp.
  - These changes were made to introduce a stronger reason to mix and match protection enchantments, rather than just use protection.
 
KNOWN ISSUES:
  - Gold tools still have their original stats. While I was able to change the tiers, I'm not sure there is an easy way to change their stats with a datapack at the moment. Please let me know if there is.
  - As a solution please use https://modrinth.com/mod/custom-durability and the config file attached. This only fixes durability.
